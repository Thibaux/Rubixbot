--- TESTING ---

- Windows -
Test the modules separately and together to see if every module is operating correctly. 
Firstly do step 1, the you can choose out of step A, B and C.

1. Run the run.bat executable in Rubick's Cube Algo

A. Kociemba -> Optiqtm
Template: ToKociemba <TEST\Input\”FILENAME”.”EXTENTION” | optiqtm
CMD code: "ToKociemba <TEST\Input\input_x.txt | optiqtm"

B. Optiqtm -> Serial
Template: ToKociemba <TEST\Input\”FILENAME”.”EXTENTION” | optiqtm >> TEST\output.txt
CMD code: "SerialCommunication COM11 <output.txt"

C. Kociemba -> Optiqtm -> Serial Communication
Template: ToKociemba <TEST\Input\”FILENAME”.”EXTENTION” | optiqtm | SerialCommunication “PORT”
CMD code: "ToKociemba <TEST\Input\input_1.txt | optiqtm | SerialCommunication COM11"
